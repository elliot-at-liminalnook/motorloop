**This is verification, not validation.** Each result is a property of
the RTL under the stated assumptions - it says nothing about whether the
RTL matches real silicon or the real motor (that needs hardware). A
**PROVEN** result is an *unbounded* proof (k-induction/PDR converged);
**BOUNDED(N)** means no counterexample within N cycles, not a proof;
**INCOMPLETE** means the proof passed but a non-vacuity cover was
unreachable (the proof may be vacuous).
